Baked Sweet Potato

    Ingredients list:
  • 4 sweet potatoes
  • kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • butter, optional
    Instructions:
  1. Preheat your oven to 400Â°F. Give your sweet potatoes a good wash and scrub.
  2. Place the sweet potatoes on a baking sheet and poke 4 to 5 holes in each sweet potato with a fork or sharp knife.
  3. Bake in the oven for approximately 60 minutes, or until soft. To test if they're done, poke them with a fork or knife. You should should feel very little resistance when inserted.
